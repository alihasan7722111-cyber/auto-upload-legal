Privacy Policy for Auto Upload

Last updated: January 2025

Auto Upload is a personal automation tool that helps the user upload short-form videos to their TikTok account. This privacy policy explains what data is collected and how it is handled.

Information We Collect

Auto Upload collects and stores only the minimum data required to upload videos to TikTok:

  • OAuth tokens (access token and refresh token) provided by TikTok after the user completes the login/authorization flow.
  • Minimal video metadata such as filename, caption, hashtags, and scheduled upload time.

How Your Data Is Used

Your data is used only to:

  • Authenticate your TikTok account through the TikTok Login Kit.
  • Upload videos to your TikTok account using the TikTok Content Posting API.

Data Storage

All collected data is stored locally on your own device in a small configuration file.

No data is sent to external servers or shared with any third parties.

Data Sharing

Auto Upload does not sell, rent, or share your data with any external services or individuals.

Data Deletion

You may delete all stored data at any time by removing the local configuration file.
